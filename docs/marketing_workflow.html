The AI Web Agency

A marketer's guide to how our business runs on autopilot.
You provide the strategy, the AI does the heavy lifting.

🧠

Human Strategy

You decide who to target and what the quality standards are.

🤖

AI Execution

The AI handles the prospecting, emailing, building, and support 24/7.

↓
Step 1: Your Job

Define the Target

You don't need to find individual businesses. You just pick the playground.

🎯

City + Niche Strategy

For example, you tell the system: "Find Plumbers in Austin, Texas." The system evaluates if this is a profitable market based on competition and demand. Once you approve it, your job here is done.

↓
Step 2: AI's Job

The Automated Hunt

While you sleep, the AI scours the internet for leads in your chosen market.

🕵️‍♂️

Finding Leads

The AI scans Google Maps to build a massive list of businesses in your target city and niche.

📉

Auditing Websites

It visits their websites and scores them. It looks for slow load times, terrible mobile design, and missing information.

📧

Finding Contacts

It automatically hunts down the correct email address for the business owner and verifies it so it won't bounce.

↓
Step 3: AI's Job

The "Soft Hook" Outreach

We never hard-sell. The AI sends a simple, irresistible question.

From: Your Agency
To: Austin Plumber

"Hey, I was looking for a plumber in Austin and noticed your website looks a bit broken on mobile phones. I actually fixed the design on my end. Want me to send you a link to see what it looks like?"

Notice: No links. No prices. Just pure curiosity. This avoids spam filters and gets responses.

↓
Step 4: AI Step 4: Human

The Demo & The Close

When the client replies "Yes, show me", the magic happens.

✨

Instant Demo Generation

The AI automatically builds a beautiful, tailored mock-up website specifically for that client's business.

👀

Human Quality Check

Before sending, you click a button to approve the demo. We never send garbage to a prospect.

💳

Checkout

The AI sends the approved demo link along with simple monthly pricing. The client checks out via Stripe/Dodo with zero human sales calls.

↓
Step 5: AI's Job

Delivery on Autopilot

They paid. Now what? The AI takes over fulfillment.

🚀

Instant Deployment

The AI connects their custom domain and pushes their new website live to the internet immediately.

💬

Ongoing Support

The AI monitors website health and handles tier-1 customer support emails. You only get notified if the client is angry or has a complex legal/billing issue.

The Result?

Scale to 50+ clients while working 5 hours a week.
