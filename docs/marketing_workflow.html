Human Strategy

AI Execution

Step 0: Your Job

Niche Hunting

You pick the playground. The AI takes over from there.

🎯

City + Niche Strategy

Before any scraping begins, you identify a profitable City+Niche pair (e.g., "Austin Roofers"). You score the market on a 100-point scale. If it passes, you click approve and the engine starts running.

↓
Steps 1 & 2: AI's Job

Lead Gen & Auditing

The AI scours the internet, builds the list, and finds the flaws.

🕵️‍♂️

1. Gather & Enrich

The AI scrapes Google Maps, gathers business info, and hunts down verified owner emails automatically.

📉

2. Audit & Score

The AI analyzes their existing websites. It looks for slow speeds, bad mobile design, and ranks leads from "Hot" to "Low" priority.

↓
Steps 3 & 4: AI's Job

The 2-Step Sales Motion

No hard selling. We generate curiosity first, demo second.

🎣

3. The "Soft Hook"

The AI sends a simple, personalized email pointing out a specific website flaw. No links. No pricing. Just: "Want to see a fixed version?"

💬

4. Interest Handling

The AI reads replies. If they say "No", it archives them. If they say "Yes", the AI instantly generates a custom demo website.

↓
Steps 5 & 6 Quality Gate

Closing & Onboarding

Converting interest into a paying retainer.

💳

5. Payment + Conversion

You approve the demo. The AI sends the link with pricing. The client checks out securely online. No sales calls needed.

📝

6. Automated Onboarding

Once paid, the AI instantly emails them to collect assets, logos, and business info needed for the final launch.

↓
Steps 7 & 8 Launch Approval

Delivery & Lifetime Support

Fulfillment on autopilot.

🚀

7. Delivery + Launch

The AI builds the final site and runs QA checks. You give the final Human Launch Approval, and the AI pushes it live.

🛡️

8. Support & Retention

The AI handles tier-1 support, ongoing minor edits, monthly reporting, and billing retries. You only step in for escalations.
