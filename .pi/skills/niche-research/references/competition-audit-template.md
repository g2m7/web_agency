# Competition Audit Template

Use this template when running Step 4 of the niche research workflow.

## Search Queries

For each niche, run these exact searches and record results:

### 1. Direct Agency Competition
- `"web design for [niche]"` — how many agencies advertise this?
- `"[niche] website design"` — any specialists?
- `"[niche] marketing agency"` — broader competition
- `"[niche] web agency"` — direct competitor search

**Record:**
| Query | Number of relevant results (first 2 pages) | Top 3 agencies found | Their offer/angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

### 2. Niche-Specific SaaS/Website Builders
- `"[niche] website builder"`
- `"[niche] website template"`
- `"[niche] website software"`

**Record:**
| Product | What it does | Price | Is it a real threat? |
|---|---|---|---|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

### 3. Owner Sentiment
Search Reddit, Google Business forums, industry forums:
- `"[niche] owner" + "website" + ("hate" OR "need" OR "looking for" OR "anyone know")`
- `"site:reddit.com [niche] website"`
- `"[niche] cold email" OR "[niche] spam"`

**Record:**
- Are owners getting pitched about websites already?
- Are they complaining about it?
- Are they asking for website help (positive signal)?

### 4. Outbound Saturation Check
- Search for cold email templates targeting this niche
- Check if any "done-for-you" outreach services target this niche
- Look on Twitter/LinkedIn for agencies bragging about closing this niche

**Record:**
- Evidence of outbound saturation: Yes/No
- If yes, how aggressive? (occasional / moderate / heavy)

## Competition Scoring

| Level | Criteria | Score |
|---|---|---|
| **None** | No agencies, no SaaS, owners not being pitched | 5 |
| **Low** | 1–2 agencies, no SaaS, minimal outbound to these owners | 4 |
| **Medium** | A few agencies, maybe 1 SaaS tool, some outbound but not heavy | 3 |
| **High** | Multiple agencies, SaaS tools exist, owners getting pitched regularly | 2 |
| **Oversaturated** | Dozens of agencies, multiple SaaS tools, owners numb to pitches | 1 |

## Red Flags

Mark a niche as **oversaturated** if ANY of these are true:
- More than 5 agencies specifically target this niche
- A niche-specific website builder exists AND is popular
- Reddit/forums show owners complaining about web design pitches
- Multiple cold email templates for this niche are publicly shared
- The niche appears in "top niches for web agencies" listicles
