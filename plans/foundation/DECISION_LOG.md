### FND-001 — Adopt 2026 market baseline before first outreach

- **Date:** `2026-04-26`
- **Status:** `ACTIVE`
- **Decision:** Use the 2025-2026 research baseline in `analysis/` to guide first-wave targeting and pricing posture. Default validation shortlist is `Phoenix + pool service`, `Charlotte + tree service`, and `Raleigh + garage door repair`. Keep launch pricing at the top of the current allowed range and stop treating the launch fee as optional after the first pilot batch.
- **Why:** The market is large enough, but generic low-price website offers are increasingly commoditized by DIY and AI builders. The research showed better early odds in high-lead-value service-area niches with visible web weakness and lower initial competition than first-wave HVAC / plumbing / roofing.
